Time-Tracking Project

Time is my closest friend and my greatest enemy: I can spend a day productively and cherish every minute, or I can waste away in a lethargic stupor and lose track of time. To combat the fading of my memories and the disconnection from my past, I started tracking how I have spent every single minute of my life every since April of 2017. The purpose of this project is not only to answer the simple question “where did my time go?”, but also to provide a valuable tool for self-reflection and self-analysis.
